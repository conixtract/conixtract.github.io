This website is hosted by GitHub Pages, a service of GitHub, Inc., 88 Colin P. Kelly Jr. Street, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA — a subsidiary of Microsoft Corporation. When you access the site, the hosting provider automatically collects and stores information that your browser transmits in server log files. This data is processed to deliver the website reliably and securely. The legal basis is Article 6 (1) (f) GDPR (legitimate interest in a functional and secure presentation of the site). 3. External resources loaded by the site
To display its design and to run, this site currently loads some resources directly from third-party servers. When such a resource is loaded, your browser connects to the relevant provider and transmits your IP address to it. The following third parties are involved:

Google Fonts
The fonts "Inter Tight" and "JetBrains Mono" are loaded from Google servers (provided by Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland, and Google LLC, USA). When you open a page, your browser requests these fonts from Google, transmitting your IP address. The purpose is consistent and appealing typography; the legal basis is Article 6 (1) (f) GDPR. A transfer to the USA may occur; Google participates in the EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework. More information: https://policies.google.com/privacy

unpkg (JavaScript libraries)
The site loads the React and Babel libraries from the unpkg content delivery network (unpkg.com). When you open a page, your browser requests these files, transmitting your IP address to the CDN operator. The purpose is the technical operation of the site; the legal basis is Article 6 (1) (f) GDPR.

4. Local storage
This site stores a single entry in your browser's local storage, named "pk-theme". It remembers whether you selected the light or dark theme. This information stays on your device, is not transmitted to me or any third party, and contains no personal data. It is used solely to provide the display preference you actively selected.

5. External links
The site links to external profiles and contact options (e.g. GitHub, ORCID, and an email link). These links only transmit data once you actively click them and are taken to the third party's website, which is then governed by that provider's own privacy policy. The email link simply opens your own email program; no data is sent to me until you choose to write.

6. Cookies and tracking
This website does not set its own cookies, and it uses no analytics, tracking, profiling, or advertising tools.

7. Your rights
Under the GDPR you have the right, in respect of your personal data, to: access (Article 15), rectification (Article 16), erasure (Article 17), restriction of processing (Article 18), data portability (Article 20), and to object to processing based on legitimate interest (Article 21 GDPR). To exercise these rights, you can contact me using the details in section 1. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority (Article 77 GDPR). The authority competent for the controller is: Landesbeauftragte für Datenschutz und Informationsfreiheit Nordrhein-Westfalen (LDI NRW), Kavalleriestraße 2-4, 40213 Düsseldorf, Germany.
